Continuing Coverage: {{tag}}

Flint’s drinking water was contaminated with lead after the city switched sources from Lake Huron to the Flint River as a way to save money in 2014. Water quality researchers at Michigan Engineering and the university are concerned about several long-term aspects of the Flint drinking water crisis and are offering their expertise and insight on the unfolding situation.
